Some simple strategy will help you deal with those tricky multiple-choice questions that you aren't sure about. First, you should understand that it's okay to guess on a multiple-choice question. In fact, it's preferable. The AP exam grading scheme no longer penalizes you for incorrect answers.

As a general rule, first see if you can eliminate one or more potential answers, and then go ahead and guess away. And, if you find there are only a few seconds left to finish several multiple choice questions, make sure you have every question filled when the time ends, even if that means guessing randomly!
