Some simple strategy will help you deal with those tricky multiple-choice questions that you aren't sure about. First, you should understand that it's okay to guess on a multiple-choice question. The AP exam grading scheme penalizes you for incorrect answers, but not as badly as you might think.

As a general rule, if you have a hunch that an answer might be right or if you can elimiate one or more potential answers, go ahead and guess away!
