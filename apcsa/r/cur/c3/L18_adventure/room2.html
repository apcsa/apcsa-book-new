When a player is moving between rooms, which method gets called first:
enterRoom()
No, this is called last, after the destination room is already determined. (This makes sense if you think about it: enterRoom must be called on the desination Room object, not on the source object!)
exitRoom()
Nope, another answer is called before this (and, in fact, calls exitRoom
tryToExit()
Yes. Looking at the Player class' actionTravel method, this is called early on.
travelDestination (from the associated Path object
Nope. This is called once it's been determined that it is OK to exit the room, in order to find out what the destination is.
