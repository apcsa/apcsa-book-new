Whoa, why are there all these different methods checking whether the player can move? Why not just have a single method that returns the next room?


In one word, the reason is inheritance. If we want to make special rooms that do complicated things, we'll make new classes that extend Room.

By having this parent class go through a specific sequence of methods, our new subclass can change what happens in specific ways by overriding one of those methods.
