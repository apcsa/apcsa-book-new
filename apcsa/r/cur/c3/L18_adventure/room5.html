Some tips for writing your GameOverRoom (you can call it anything you want, of course):


You'll want the overridden enterRoom method to print out some information about why the game is suddenly ending. Since entering this room always ends the game, you could do this in the description.

Think about whether you should do this with a call to super.enterRoom() or simply a call to printDescription. Both can work.


Your new class will need a World instance variable so that it can call the gameOver method. This means you will need a new constructor. Remember, you need to call super() as the first statement in your new constructor method.


Don't forget to add your room with the World constructor, attach it to an existing room using the addExit method, and test it thoroughly!
