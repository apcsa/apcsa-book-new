Open the Sound class now, and look at it. There are several methods that you will use in this lesson. Read the comments to understand how you will use them. Some of the methods duplicate the behaviors you can get from the viewer buttons (e.g., play).

One important method is refresh. In this lesson, you are going to write your own methods that make changes to the integers in the sound's ArrayList. However, the viewer won't know you've changed them unless you invoke the refresh method. Only then will the picture update and reflect the new sample values.
