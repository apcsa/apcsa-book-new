At the top of the viewer is a miniature image of the entire sound waveform. The numbers at the bottom are seconds, and show that this birdsong is about 2 and a half seconds long. The ArrayList for this sound holds about 55,000 integers, therefore.

Let's zoom in to take a look. Click your mouse button down about half way along the large image, and drag it to the right about a quarter of the way to the right edge of the window.



After selecting the "Zoom In" button in the view, the viewer window will give you a closer look at the portion that you selected. Look at the miniature waveform at the top: the green band shows you the zoomed in portion of the current sound.
