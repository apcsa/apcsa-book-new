Consider the following declaration:
ArrayList<Name> friends = new ArrayList<Name>();

We are trying to insert items into our ArrayList. Which of the following will NOT work:
friends.add(new Name("Ciara","Smith"));
No, this will work, we are creating an instance of the Name object and adding it to the list
