Classify a poker hand. There are several categories, listed below from least to most likely.

  1. Royal flush: 10 J Q K A, all the same suit.
  2. Straight flush: five consecutive cards, all the same suit.
  3. Four of a kind: all four cards with a given rank.
  4. Flush: all five cards in the same suit.
  5. Straight: five cards with consecutive ranks, for example, 5 2 4 6 3.
  6. Three of a kind: three cards of the same rank.
  7. Two pair: two cards with the same rank, and another two cards with a different rank.
  8. One pair: two cards with the same rank.
  9. A hand with none of the above.

You should use a Hand class that has a handType method. handType should return a corresponding int chosen from the list above.
