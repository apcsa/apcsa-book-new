Here's another sorting algorithm called insertion sort. Translated into cards and decks, it works as follows.

  1. Initialize an ArrayList named sortedSoFar to empty.
    1. Choose and remove the first element from myCards.
    2. Insert it into sortedSoFar at its proper position.
    3. Repeat this process until no more elements remain in the unsorted myCards.
  3. Assign sortedSoFar to myCards.
