Evaluating the exchange-based shuffle

How good is the shuffling-by-exchange technique? Briefly explain your response.

There are a lot of ways for the deck to remain unchanged. After one exchange, there are three ways out of six; after two exchanges, there are 21 out of 81 ways to get the deck we started with. So that's not good.
I wrote some code to simulate this, and the starting order of the deck came up more than anything else did.
