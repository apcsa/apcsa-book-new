Experiments with insertion sort

After adding the comparisonCount variable to the Deck class as specified in the preceding step, test the insertionSort method on three states of the Deck: a deck already in sorted; a deck just initialized so it is in suit-rank order; and a deck that is shuffled. Explain the results.

I got 3 comparisons for an already ordered deck, 6 for reverse order, and 4 for CBAD. If everything is in order, it only goes through the deck once.
My comparison count turned out to be always between 3 and 6. When the deck is in order, you get three comparisons; when it's backward, you get 6.
