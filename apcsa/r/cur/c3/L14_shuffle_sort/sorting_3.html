Comparison of sorting methods (2)

Over 20,000 students take the AP CS exam each year. What sorting method would you use to sort the exams alphabetically by the name of the test taker: insertion sort, selection sort, or something else? Briefly justify your choice.

I assume each exam has an ID number. I would sort the exams into groups based on the first digit of its ID, then sort each group by the second digit, and so on.
I wouldn't use insertion sort or selection sort; those would be too slow for 20000 exams. How about if we divided them in half, and sorted smaller piles, and then put them together?
