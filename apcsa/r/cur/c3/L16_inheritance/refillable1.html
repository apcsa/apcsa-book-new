Here's how we say that CokeCooler is just like Refillable.

public class CokeCooler extends Refillable {
}

With that declaration, all the public methods of Refillable are also available to CokeCooler objects without needing a reference to Refillable itself. We refer to CokeCooler as a subclass or child class of Refillable, and to Refillable as a superclass or parent class of CokeCooler.

We also say that CokeCooler inherits from Refillable.




Similarly, classes like CandyJar and. say, OxygenTank could also inherit from Refillable.
