Now that we have a CandyJar object, we can start calling its methods.



  1. Eat three of the candies in the jar. Do this by right-clicking (or, on a Macintosh, command-clicking) on candyJar1 to call its eat method, supplying an argument of 3.

  2. Eat two more pieces of candy.

  3. The candy jar should now be empty.

  4. Call the candyCount method to verify this.
