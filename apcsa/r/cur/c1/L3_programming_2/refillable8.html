Other refillable objects?

We've discussed candy jars, soda machines, and score keeping. Name two other objects in the real-world that are refillable. You might consult with the Refillable class in the previous step, to see the behaviors and methods that it has.

Example 1: bank account. My bank account increases or decreases depending on whether I deposit or withdraw money. Example 2: My stapler use up staples when I staple papers, but the amount of staples can be refilled if I put more staples in.
I can think of objects in real life that are refillable: 1. Water bottle. I decrease the amount when I drink water, and refill it at a tap when I need more water. 2. Bookshelf. I can take books out of it and put books into it. In the Refillable class however, I won't be able to differentiate between the books.
