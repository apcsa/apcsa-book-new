Cause of the bug

Suppose you met the programmer whose mistyping produced the bug, and you asked him or her "Why did you make this error?" What would the programmer say?

He or she would say, "Oops, I made a typo! He he!"
She might say "I often get comparisons backwards".
The programmer might complain about how close < and > are on the keyboard, so it's easy to hit one when you mean another.
