You will need to make use of the the % operator, also called modulus. It computes the remainder when dividing the first operand by the second. For example, year % 4 divides year by 4 and returns the remainder, which will be one of 0, 1, 2, or 3. If year is 5, year % 4 would return 1. You can get more information from the glossary entry for "modulus".
