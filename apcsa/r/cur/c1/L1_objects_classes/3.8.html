Suppose we want to represent volume on a portable music player (i.e. how loud or soft the music is playing). How would you do this? Can you represent it in more than one way? Explain your answer.

Well, for detailed information about the state of the volume I'd use a number.
A number is the best variable for the state of the volume, but I also might use a Boolean to track if this is the Earbud or External Speaker volume setting (an either/or situation)
I would use a number, with big numbers representing loud and small numbers representing low volume.
