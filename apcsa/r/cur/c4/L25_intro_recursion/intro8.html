If you're working in a class of at least five people, you can act out a recursive method. Form a line of four people, and give each of the four a copy of the following.

  1. Wait for the person ahead of you in line to tell you a string.
  2. If he or she tells you the empty string, tell him or her 0.
  3. Otherwise, tell the next person in line the string that results from removing the first character in the string you were told. Wait for that person to tell you a number. Then add 1 to that number, and tell it to the person who originally told you the string.
