In this exercise you will create a mirror image from a file. To do this, you need to modify one half of the picture so that it shows a 'reflection' of the other half. It will look as if half a picture is being held up to a mirror.

To do this, you will be throwing out half of the information in the picture. And, you won't see any effect when pictures are already symmetric.


Create a method called mirror that transforms the image so that the left side is a mirror image of the right side.

Faces are particularly interesting when made into mirror images, although you may need to adjust where the center line is.
