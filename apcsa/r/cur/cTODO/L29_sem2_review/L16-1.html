New Terms:

  • Inheritance
  • subclass
  • superclass
  • extends
  • overriding
  • hierarchy
  • polymorphism

Key Concepts:

  • A subclass inherits from a superclass by using the extends keyword.
  • Objects of a subclass have access to methods and variables in the superclass, assuming that they are public.
  • A subclass can override a method from the superclass. This means to replace that method with one of its own.
  • At runtime, Java will choose the most specialized version of a method to run. In other words, it will choose the version of a method furthest down in an object's hierarchy.
  • The ability of Java to determine what method to use at runtime is called polymorphism.
