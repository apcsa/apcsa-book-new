Start up the GridWorld program by executing the main method of the BugRunner class. Click Step several times, then Run, then Stop when you've seen enough.

The initial environment contains only a bug and a rock. To explore the program more thoroughly, you should create more objects. Do this by clicking on an empty square of the grid, and selecting the constructor of the object you wish to create.

You can also affect the behavior of an existing object by clicking on it and then selecting one of its methods.


Part 1 of the case study narrative describes the environment and the objects in it in more detail. Read this part before proceeding.
