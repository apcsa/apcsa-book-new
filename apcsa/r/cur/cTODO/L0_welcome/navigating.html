You can move between the pages in a lesson by clicking the green "Next" arrow (top bar) or by clicking directly on any page in the left navigation menu.

The left navigation menu shows the Page within each Section (blue bars). You can close a completed Section by clicking on its blue bar.

To learn more about the structure of each course lesson, click the link in the Information box to the right (see icon).

The Audio Controller:
You can use the audio controller to turn audio playback on or off. Most of the text displayed in this course has corresponding audio -- except for long excerpts of program code and some other special cases.

To play or pause the audio click the icon. A paragraph of text will highlight and the matching audio will play.

Click the Forward icon to skip forward through the text, one paragraph at a time.

Click the Rewind icon to skip backwards through the text, one paragraph at a time.
