Alternative goatDoorToShow

Suppose goatDoorToShow always returns the first door with a goat rather than a random door with a goat. What is the effect of this change on the ability of the contestant to win?

I think it will act the same as the original, even though the selection is a little less "random".
If I knew that Monty would always show me the first door with a goat, then if Monty showed me door number 2, I know the car is in door #1. So if that change is made and I know about it, I have better odds for the car.
