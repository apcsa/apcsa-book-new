Open the current project in BlueJ (lesson9\Date1).

The constructor takes a day of the year in a leap year as argument, and determines the corresponding month number (a number between 1 and 12, stored in myMonthNumber) and day in that month (stored in myDateInMonth). For example, the argument 30 should set myMonthNumber to 1 and myDateInMonth to 30, and the argument 32 should set myMonthNumber to 2 and myDateInMonth to 1.
