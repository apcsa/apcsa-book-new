Background

The game of Hangman is played by two people, a "chooser" and a "guesser". The chooser thinks of a word, and write a row of dashes, one per character in the word: 

   _ _ _ _ _ _ _

The guesser tries to guess the word by asking if it contains a particular character:

"Does it have an R?"

If the guesser asked about a letter contained in the word, the chooser writes it in the corresponding positions in the row of dashes: 

   R _ _ _ _ R _

The guesser wins by guessing all the characters in the word with fewer than a predetermined number of wrong guesses.

The game is called "Hangman" because the chooser draws a portion of a diagram of a hanging person at each incorrect guess made by the guesser.

Problem

Code and test a version of the Hangman game. Your solution will involve a Hangman class whose constructor chooses a word, and whose guess method processes each guessed character.
