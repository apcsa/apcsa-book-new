Write a method named nextLeapYear that, given an integer year, returns the first leap year that's greater than year.

Leap years are commonly thought to occur every 4 years, but there are some exceptions to this rule. If a year is divisible by 400, then it is a leap year. If it is divisible by 100, it is not a leap year. Otherwise, if it is divisible by 4, then it is a leap year. Here are some examples:

YearLeap year?
1800 No
1900 No
1904 Yes
1905 No
2000 Yes
