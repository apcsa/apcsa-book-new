Explore the following simulation of the I like Java! while loop. When you click "Execute" once, or "Step" repeatedly, you will see a purple highlight moving through the lines of the program. The highlight indicates what statement is getting executed. After the statement is executed, any effect that it has is also shown in the simulation (e.g., printing, adding to the count variable, etc).

You can change the value of count during execution.



To view this page ensure that Adobe Flash Player version 10.0.0 or greater is installed.
