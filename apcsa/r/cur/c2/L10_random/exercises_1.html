Write a program to play the casino dice game named Craps. Here are the rules.

  1. The player bets, then rolls a pair of dice. For this simulation, don't worry about the size of the bet.
  2. If the roll is 2, 3, or 12, the player loses the bet.
  3. If the roll is 7 or 11, the player wins the bet.
  4. Otherwise, the roll becomes the point.
  5. The player continues to roll until rolling either 7—for which the player loses—or rolling the point—for which the player wins.

Simulate the game many, many times and determine how often, as a percentage, the player is likely to win his or her bet.
