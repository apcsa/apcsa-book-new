The way to look at many, many rolls is with a chart. The lesson10/Dice2 project contains a bar chart class that can show the roll of a single die. A good random number generator will make a uniform distribution, or a set of numbers in which any particular number is as likely as any other.

Open the current project in BlueJ, and use the DieRollGenerator and BarChart6 classes to look at many, many rolls of your dice generator. (lesson10/Dice2).
