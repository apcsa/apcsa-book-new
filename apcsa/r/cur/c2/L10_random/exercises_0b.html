Two additional points:


To make things exciting, you should make it so that one dot can surge ahead of the others. This isn't likely to happen with many trials from a uniform random number generator. (Try it, though).

Instead, you should, once in a while, move the dot multiple pixels, and very occasionally, move the dot ahead a lot. Play with this to find the most interesting effect.


Some people at ball games swear that certain dots win more often than others. Make it so! Look at the Dot class and figure out how to make a dot that can cheat. (Don't do it in the DotRacer methods, where other dots might be looking ...).

You might need to make another class like Dot, say, called CheaterDot, that has the same methods as Dot (albeit with a sneaky change or two to a method body). Be subtle, however (i.e., use randomness).
