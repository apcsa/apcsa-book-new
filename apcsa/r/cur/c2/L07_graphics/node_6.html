Pixels?

Look at the pixels on your computer screen. How many pixels across are displayed on it? Explain how you determined that number.

I think about 50. It is hard to tell. What I did is I opened notepad and typed some periods, but then I saw when I put the cursor over the period that each was actually two pixels wide. I guess my cursor is one pixel wide! Anyway, I moved the cursor just a little bit until it shifted over, and that was one pixel. There were 7 pixels between each period, and about 7 periods in an inch of the screen. Yeah!
Unlike Mario's crazy way, I measured the width of my screen, and then looked up what resolution it was. My screen resolution is 1280 dots wide, and it is like 20 inches across. Then I used a calculator because I can't divide in my head. 1280/20 is 64, so 64 pixels per inch.
I used a ruler to measure the size of the 300-by-400 pixel window. It's around 100 pixels per inch.
