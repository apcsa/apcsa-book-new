Suppose k1 is the position of a card with rank 9, and k2 is the position of a card with rank 2, with k1 < k2. Which of the following successfully removes the two cards from myCards?

myCards.remove (k1); 
myCards.remove (k2);
When the card at position k1 is removed, all subsequent cards move down one position. 
myCards.remove (k2);
myCards.remove (k1);
Yes. This removes the element that is later in the list first allowing us to then remove element 2 and then all subsequent cards move down one position.
