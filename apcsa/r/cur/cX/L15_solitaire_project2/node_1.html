Open the current project in BlueJ (lesson15\elevens).

You now have two tasks to complete.

  1. Provide a method named play that plays a game of Elevens and returns an int: 1 if the game was won, 0 if it was lost.

  2. Your task is to determine the value of k such that at least one quarter (25%) of the games are winning ones.

    Specifically, you should find the least value of k such that, when 500 games of Elevens are played using that value of k, at least 125 of the games are won.

This may seem like a large assignment. However, you've already dealt with some of its components:

  • activities with decks of cards in lessons 12 and 14;
  • the Monty Hall simulation in lesson 11.

Don't be reluctant to reuse code from these activities.
