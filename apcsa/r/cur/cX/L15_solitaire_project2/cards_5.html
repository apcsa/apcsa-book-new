A solitaire game named Elevens (The Complete Book of Solitaire and Patience Games, by Albert H. Morehead and Geoffrey Mott-Smith, Bantam Books, 1966) is played as follows.

  1. Deal nine cards. (This will be the hand.)
  2. Repeat until no more cards remain.
    1. Remove pairs of cards whose ranks add to 11 (an ace's rank is 1 in this game).
    2. Remove face cards (ranks 11, 12, and 13) in groups of three.
    3. Deal cards to the hand to replace the ones that were removed.

To remove cards from the hand, we will use the ArrayList remove method. remove takes a position as argument. It removes the element at the given position, moving all following elements down one position/ The diagrams below show the result of executing remove(3).
